Bio

I am a tech staff member at MIT Lincoln Laboratory. Prior to this, I graduated with a PhD from the MIT/WHOI Joint Program. There, I worked with Prof. John Leonard and Dr. Julien Bonnel on novel machine learning methods for recovery of seafloor acoustic properties and underwater source localization. Before starting my PhD, I've received a M.Sc. in Energy Engineering from the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology where I worked with Prof. Guy Ramon on a novel adsorption based thermo-acoustic refrigeration technology.
