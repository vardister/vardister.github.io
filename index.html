Ariel Vardi

I am a PhD Candidate at the MIT/WHOI Joint-Program. I am working with Prof. John Leonard at MIT and Dr. Julien Bonnel at WHOI on combining underwater acoustics with modern machine learning tools for recovery of sea floor acoustic properties. Prior to starting my Ph.D., I've received a MS.C in Energy Engineering from the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology where I worked with Prof. Guy Ramon on a novel adsorption based thermoacoustic refrigiration technology. I am curious about the intersection of acoustics, signal processing, and machine learning, and I am looking for opportunities to apply these tools to solve challenging problems in oceanography and environmental monitoring.
