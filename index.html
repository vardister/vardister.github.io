Bio

I am a recent PhD graduate from the MIT/WHOI Joint-Program. During my PhD, I worked with Prof. John Leonard and Dr. Julien Bonnel on novel machine learning methods for recovery of seafloor acoustic properties and underwater source localization. Prior to starting my Ph.D., I've received a MS.C in Energy Engineering from the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology where I worked with Prof. Guy Ramon on a novel adsorption based thermo-acoustic refrigeration technology. Currently, I am actively looking for new opportunities. I am curious about the intersection of acoustics, signal processing, and machine learning, and I am looking for interesting problems to apply these tools to solve challenging problems in oceanography and environmental monitoring.
